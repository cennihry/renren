My love, My Astra


From afar, I watch you like a distant star,
Your radiance reaching me from afar.
In the silence of the night, I send my love,
Hoping it'll reach you like a gentle dove.

So I'll continue to love you from afar,
Admiring you like a distant star.
For in my heart, you'll forever reside,
My love for you, an eternal tide.

Icen Hyrvinz D.Tusit
11-Agoncillo
